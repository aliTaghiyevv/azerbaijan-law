# Citation Style

Use citations that let a reader find the exact Azerbaijan source.

## Minimum Citation

Include:

- Act or decision title.
- Act/decision number if available.
- Date adopted or issued.
- Article, clause, paragraph, or section.
- Version or amendment date when relevant.
- Official source URL.

## Citation Examples

Use compact citations in quick answers:

`Civil Code of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Art. X, as amended through [date], [official URL].`

`Decision No. [number] of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Azerbaijan, [date], para. [x], [official URL].`

`Constitutional Court of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Decision [title/number], [date], [official URL].`

## Translation Notes

If using English text from a non-official or machine translation, mark it as unofficial:

`Unofficial English translation of Azerbaijani original.`

If exact wording matters, quote short excerpts from the official Azerbaijani text and then translate or summarize.

## Source List

End research memos with a `Sources` section. Prefer source links over bare text references.
