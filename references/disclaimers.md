# Disclaimers

Use concise disclaimers. Do not overdo them.

## Standard Wording

For most answers:

`This is legal information and research support, not legal advice from an Azerbaijan-licensed lawyer.`

For high-stakes matters:

`Because this affects legal rights, obligations, deadlines, or liability, have an Azerbaijan-licensed lawyer review the facts and current law before acting.`

For translations:

`The translation is unofficial unless an official translation is cited.`

## When To Escalate

Recommend qualified legal counsel when the task involves:

- Litigation strategy or court filing.
- Criminal, administrative offense, sanctions, or enforcement exposure.
- Tax liability or audit response.
- Employment termination or immigration status.
- Corporate restructuring, licensing, securities, banking, insurance, or regulated activity.
- Real estate title or notarization.
- Deadlines, limitation periods, appeals, penalties, or government submissions.
- User asks whether they should take a legally consequential action.
