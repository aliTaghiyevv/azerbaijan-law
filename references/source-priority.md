# Source Priority

Use official Azerbaijan government and court sources before secondary sources.

## Primary Sources

Prefer these sources when available:

1. Official legal acts database: `e-qanun.az` or official successor/AI interface.
2. Ministry of Justice: `justice.gov.az`.
3. President of the Republic of Azerbaijan official legal publications when relevant.
4. Cabinet of Ministers official materials when relevant.
5. Supreme Court: `supremecourt.gov.az`.
6. Constitutional Court: `constcourt.gov.az`.
7. Regulator websites for sector rules, such as the Central Bank for banking and finance.

## Secondary Sources

Use law-firm articles, blogs, commercial databases, commentary, and unofficial translations only for orientation. Verify the rule against official text before presenting it as law.

## Source Capture

Record these fields whenever possible:

- Official title of the act or decision.
- Act number, decision number, or case identifier.
- Adoption date.
- Effective date.
- Latest amendment or version date.
- Article, chapter, clause, paragraph, or section.
- URL and access date when useful.

## Recency Rule

If the user's request concerns current legal rights, obligations, deadlines, penalties, registration, licensing, tax, employment, immigration, corporate, finance, public procurement, litigation, or regulatory compliance, verify current law before answering.
