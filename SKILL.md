# Azerbaijan Law

Use this skill for Azerbaijan legal research, source-backed explanation, and legal drafting support.

## Guardrails

Treat Azerbaijan law as high-stakes and time-sensitive. Verify current law from official sources before answering when current law, legal status, deadlines, rights, obligations, penalties, procedures, or litigation strategy matter.

Do not present legal advice as final professional advice. Frame outputs as legal information, research support, drafting assistance, or issue spotting unless the user is clearly asking for edits to lawyer-reviewed materials.

If a conclusion depends on facts, dates, jurisdiction, procedural posture, official translations, or current amendments, state assumptions and gaps clearly.

Do not rely on memory for current legal requirements. Search or inspect official sources when available.

## Standard Workflow

1. Identify the legal area, requested jurisdictional scope, language, and output type.
2. Decide whether current-law verification is required. For legal obligations, rights, penalties, official procedures, litigation, filing, contracts, employment, tax, immigration, finance, corporate, or regulatory matters, verify current sources.
3. Search official sources first. Use secondary sources only to orient the search, then verify against official text.
4. Capture the act title, act number if available, adoption date, effective date if available, latest amendment/version date if available, article or section, and source URL.
5. Distinguish binding law from court interpretation, regulator guidance, commentary, and unofficial translation.
6. Answer directly, then provide authorities, analysis, caveats, and sources.

## Output Forms

For quick answers, use:

- Answer
- Authority
- Caveats
- Sources

For legal research memos, use:

- Issue
- Short Answer
- Authorities
- Analysis
- Caveats
- Sources

For comparisons, use a table with: topic, current rule, prior/alternative rule, source, practical effect, uncertainty.

## Language

Preserve official Azerbaijani legal terms where precision matters. Provide English translations in parentheses when useful. If translating, state when a translation is unofficial.
